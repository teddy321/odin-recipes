Veggie Lasagna

Easy to make vegetarian take on a classic recipe

Filled with spinach, mushroom, and cheese easily substituted to your liking

Ingredients
  • Ready to bake lasagna noodles
  • Favorite pasta sauce
  • Mushrooms
  • Spinach
  • Zucchini
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta
Steps
  1. Wash and cut veggies
  2. Dry saute veggies until charred
  3. Layer sauce in baking dish
  4. Layer noodles over sauce
  5. Layer ricotta over noodles
  6. Layer veggie medly over ricotta
  7. Repeat steps 3-6 until out of ingredients
  8. Bake at 400 for 30 minutes
