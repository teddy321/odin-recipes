Enchiladas

Hearty and delicious Mexican meal

Loaded with flavor and cheese

Ingredients
  • Brisket
  • Chipotles Adobo
  • Veggie Stock
  • Black Beans
  • Corn
  • Tortillas
  • Enchilada Sauce
  • Cheese
Steps
  1. Season brisket and sear
  2. Coat brisket in blended chipotles and adobo, place in slow cooker on low with veggie stock for 8 hours
  3. Shred brisket and mix with black beans and corn
  4. Put filling in tortillas in baking dish
  5. Layer with sauce and cheese
  6. Bake at 375 for 30 minutes
